### **Faction: Interstellar Venture Organization (IVO)**  
**Reputation**: Neutral-good adventurer’s guild. Officially ethical, but individuals within it might cut corners.  
**Unofficial Nicknames**: *Rift Riders* (by nobles), *Star Chasers* (by conscripts).  
**Core Rules**:  
- No exploitation of sentient rift-entities.  
- Fair profit-sharing with contractors.  
- Strict bans on trafficking Abyss Walkers.  

**Key Characters**:  
1. **CEO Rylan Voss** (50s): Charismatic idealist. Wants to “unite the moon through exploration.” Secretly dying of rift-poisoning.  
2. **Kestrel** (30s): Paragon Captain. Wields *Starbreaker* (Tier 5 plasma glaive). Refuses shady jobs. Mentor figure to recruits.  
3. **Dr. Elara Valzvein** (40s): Rogue biologist. Secretly sells Abyss Walker tissue to Eclipse Syndicate (her own side hustle).  
4. **Jinx** (20s): Chaotic freelancer. Uses *Chaos Dice* (Tier 4). Hired for deniable ops. Calls IVO “Space Boy Scouts.”  
5. **The Auditor**: IVO’s AI. Enforces rules but turns a blind eye to *personal* misconduct.  

**Ties**:  
- Contracts with **Lunar Military** for rift-scouting.  
- Hires **“Drifters”** (freelance mercenaries) for risky jobs.  
- Feuds with **Eclipse Syndicate** (black market rivals).  

---

### **Faction: The Afflicted (“Abyss Walkers”)**  
**Unofficial Terms**: *Void-Touched* (military term), *Glowies* (derogatory slang).  
**Description**: Humans mutated by rift exposure. Skin turns translucent blue; veins glow. Most lose sanity, but a few retain consciousness.  

**Subgroups**:  
1. **The Hollowed**: Feral, mindless Afflicted. Roam rifts in packs.  
2. **The Ascended**: Rare lucid Afflicted. Hide in rift-dimension settlements.  
3. **The Whispering Tide**: Cultists who *embrace* mutations. Worship “the Voice” (Weeping Star).  

**Key Characters**:  
1. **Nyx** (Unknown): Ascended Abyss Walker. Helps conscripts escape the military. Wears a cloak of void-mist.  
2. **Vesper** (20s): Whispering Tide leader. Can open mini-rifts with her voice. Wants to “free” the Weeping Star.  
3. **Warden** (50s): Ex-soldier turned Afflicted vigilante. Hunts corrupt Overseers.  

---

### **Faction: Eclipse Syndicate (Unofficial Name)**  
**Reputation**: Black market conglomerate. Nobles pretend it doesn’t exist.  
**Unofficial Nicknames**: *The Shadow Exchange*, *Void Traders*.  
**Key Characters**:  
1. **“Silk”** (30s): Smuggler kingpin. Sells everything from rift-serums to Afflicted body parts.  
2. **Dr. Vex** (40s): Mad scientist. Creates hybrid Afflicted for the syndicate.  
3. **Mirage** (25): Illusionist thief. Uses a **Tier 3 artifact *Phantom Veil*** to infiltrate IVO.  
4. **The Twins**: Conjoined hackers. Sell rift-data to the highest bidder.  
5. **The Faceless**: Syndicate enforcer. Identity unknown; rumors say they’re a noble.  

**Ties**:  
- Buys IVO’s leaked data from Jinx.  
- Supplies **Shattered Hand** mercenaries with rift-weapons.

**Locations**:
